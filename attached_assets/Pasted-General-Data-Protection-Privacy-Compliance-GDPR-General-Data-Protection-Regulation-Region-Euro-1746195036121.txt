General Data Protection & Privacy Compliance
GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)

Region: European Union

Purpose: Protect personal data privacy.

CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)

Region: California, USA

Purpose: Personal data privacy protection and consumer rights.

HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act)

Region: United States

Purpose: Health data privacy and security.

2. Financial Sector Compliance
PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard)

Region: Global

Purpose: Security of credit/debit card transactions.

GLBA (Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act)

Region: United States

Purpose: Privacy of consumer financial information.

SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley Act)

Region: United States

Purpose: Financial transparency and accuracy.

3. Cybersecurity and Information Security Compliance
ISO/IEC 27001

Region: International

Purpose: Comprehensive information security management.

SOC 1, SOC 2, SOC 3 (System and Organization Controls)

Region: Global

Purpose: Controls relevant to security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy.

NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)

Region: United States, widely adopted internationally

Purpose: Standardized cybersecurity risk management framework.

NIST SP 800-53 & NIST SP 800-171

Region: Primarily U.S. government and contractors

Purpose: Information security controls for federal agencies and contractors.

CMMC (Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification)

Region: U.S. Department of Defense contractors

Purpose: Protect federal contract information and controlled unclassified information.

CIS Controls (Center for Internet Security)

Region: Global

Purpose: Prioritized cybersecurity best practices.

4. Healthcare and Life Sciences Compliance
HITECH Act (Health Information Technology for Economic and Clinical Health Act)

Region: United States

Purpose: Enhances HIPAA, encouraging electronic health records and security standards.

FDA 21 CFR Part 11

Region: United States

Purpose: Electronic records and signatures compliance for FDA-regulated industries.

5. Government and Public Sector Compliance
FedRAMP (Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program)

Region: U.S. Federal Agencies

Purpose: Security assessment for cloud services used by the government.

FISMA (Federal Information Security Management Act)

Region: U.S. Federal Government

Purpose: Information security for federal data and IT systems.

6. Education Sector Compliance
FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act)

Region: United States

Purpose: Privacy and security of student educational records.

7. Cloud Security Compliance
CSA STAR (Cloud Security Alliance - Security Trust Assurance and Risk)

Region: Global

Purpose: Assurance and transparency for cloud computing security.

8. Industry-Specific & Regional Compliance
COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act)

Region: United States

Purpose: Protecting the privacy of children online.

PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act)

Region: Canada

Purpose: Personal data privacy and electronic records protection.

LGPD (Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados)

Region: Brazil

Purpose: Data protection and privacy law similar to GDPR.

Australia Privacy Act (APP - Australian Privacy Principles)

Region: Australia

Purpose: Personal information management and protection.

9. Environmental & Social Compliance (often relevant to IT organizations)
ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) standards

Region: Global

Purpose: Sustainability and corporate social responsibility reporting.

Additional Best-Practice Frameworks (not compliance per se, but often adopted):
ITIL (Information Technology Infrastructure Library)

COBIT (Control Objectives for Information and Related Technologies)

OWASP (Open Web Application Security Project) Top 10
